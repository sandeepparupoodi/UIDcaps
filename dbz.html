📖 Dragon Ball Z – Encyclopedia Entry

🏷️ Basic Information

  • Japanese Title: Dragon Ball Z
  • English Title: Dragon Ball Z
  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Martial Arts, Fantasy

📜 Plot Summary

Goku, a powerful warrior from another planet, protects Earth from powerful enemies.

With the help of his friends, he battles villains and unlocks stronger transformations to save the world.

👥 Main Characters

Goku Vegeta Gohan Piccolo Frieza
