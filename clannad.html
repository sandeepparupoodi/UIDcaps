📖 Clannad – Encyclopedia Entry

🏷️ Basic Information

  • Japanese Title: Clannad
  • English Title: Clannad
  • Genre: Romance, Drama, Slice of Life
  • Episodes: 47 (Clannad + Clannad After Story)
  • Release Year: 2007
  • Studio: Kyoto Animation

📜 Plot Summary

Tomoya Okazaki is a high school student who feels life is meaningless until he meets Nagisa Furukawa.

Through friendship, love, and family struggles, Tomoya learns the importance of relationships and finds new purpose in life.

👥 Main Characters

Tomoya Okazaki Nagisa Furukawa Tomoyo Sakagami Kyou Fujibayashi Fuko Ibuki

🏯 Cultural Impact

  • Memorable character arcs
