  • Release Year: 2016

📜 Plot Summary

Subaru Natsuki is suddenly transported to a fantasy world with no explanation.

After being killed, he discovers he has the ability to return to a previous point in time whenever he dies, forcing him to relive tragic events to protect those he cares about.
