📜 Plot Summary

Gabimaru the Hollow is a powerful ninja sentenced to death. He is offered a chance at freedom if he retrieves the legendary Elixir of Life.

Along with other criminals and executioners, he travels to a dangerous island filled with deadly creatures and mysterious powers.
