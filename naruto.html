  • Release Year: 2002
  • Studio: Studio Pierrot

📜 Plot Summary

Naruto Uzumaki is a mischievous ninja who dreams of becoming Hokage, the strongest leader of his village.

Despite being feared by villagers because of the Nine-Tailed Fox sealed inside him, Naruto trains hard, builds friendships, and protects his village from dangerous enemies.
