📜 Plot Summary

Tanjiro Kamado lives peacefully with his family until demons attack and kill them. His sister Nezuko survives but is turned into a demon.

Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps to find a cure for Nezuko and defeat powerful demons threatening humanity.
