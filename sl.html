📜 Plot Summary

Hunters protect humanity from monsters emerging through mysterious gates. Sung Jin-Woo is known as the weakest hunter.

After surviving a deadly dungeon, he gains a special system that allows him to grow stronger without limits, becoming humanity's strongest warrior.
